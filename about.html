ℹ️ About Agri-Allied

Our Mission

Agri-Allied is designed to help farmers access important agricultural information in one place. Our goal is to support farmers with crop guidance, weather updates, government schemes, and farming resources.

Our Vision

To create a smart and farmer-friendly platform that improves agricultural productivity and helps farmers make informed decisions.

Services Provided

  • 🌾 Crop Information
  • 🌦 Weather Updates
  • 🏛 Government Schemes
  • 🛒 Farmer Marketplace
  • 🌱 Soil Health Tips
  • 🐛 Crop Disease Detection

Project Information

Agri-Allied is a web application developed as an agricultural support platform using HTML, CSS, Node.js, Express.js, and MySQL.
